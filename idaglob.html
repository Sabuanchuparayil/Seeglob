What is IdaGlob?

IdaGlob is a revolutionary B2B travel marketplace that connects travel agents, tour operators, and destination management companies worldwide. Our platform enables the seamless creation, distribution, and management of travel itineraries while providing powerful tools for revenue optimization and customer engagement.

From small independent agents to large tour operators, IdaGlob provides the technology infrastructure needed to thrive in today's digital travel industry.
