What is KeyShare?

KeyShare is an innovative, comprehensive property management solution designed to meet the dynamic needs of today's real estate market. It provides a unified platform for real estate agents, caretakers, and property owners to efficiently manage their rental portfolios.

Our mission is to simplify complex property operations, fostering seamless communication and ensuring optimal asset performance. From tenant onboarding and automated rent collection to maintenance management and insightful financial reporting, KeyShare empowers you to take control of your properties with ease. Experience a new era of efficiency and transparency in property management.
